THE BIGGEST
PANDEMICS IN HISTORY

At the end of 2019, we were surprised by the discovery of a virus that causes a disease that in a short time made the WHO (World Health Organization) declare it a pandemic and forced the entire world to stop and rethink what is health behavior. COVID-19 was the subject of 2020, which will forever be known as the year of lockdown, masks, distance and mourning. In Brazil, we face, in addition to the coronavirus, the denial and irresponsibility of government officials and citizens who did not believe in the disease's potential. As a result, by 2021 we already have an impressive 500,000 deaths mark.

The coronavirus is already considered to be one of the great pandemics that humanity has ever known. But do you know what the other? Click on the names below and learn more about them.
